<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Hedera offer a funding program for application developers?">
    Hedera and the Hedera Foundation offer various funding programs and grants to support developers building on the Hedera network. Developers can apply for grants through the [Hedera Foundation](https://hedera.foundation/) and other ecosystem [grants and accelerator programs](https://hedera.com/ecosystem/grants-accelerators).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a Hedera community chat for developers?">
    Yes, Hedera has an active developer community on [Discord](http://hedera.com/discord) where developers can ask questions, share projects, and connect with other builders. Technical discussions and support are available through this community channel.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
